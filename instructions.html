You get 5 rounds of the game. During each round, you choose rock, paper, or scissors by selecting from the images below; and the computer(your opponent) randomnly selects from the 3 options as well. what you or the computer select(s), you or the computer get a point. The chart below shows who gets a point based on who selects what

* If you chose Rock If you chose Paper If you chose scissors
If the computer chooses Rock Tie! You win! Paper covers rock! Computer Wins! Rock breaks scissors
If the computer chooses Paper Computer wins! Paper covers rock! Tie! You win! Scissors cuts paper!
If the computer chooses Scissors You win! Rock breaks scissors! Computer wins! Scissors cuts paper! Tied!

The round number is displayed above the images, and the scores are displayed below the images for you to see. After 5 rounds, the game ends and the one that gets the most points wins.
